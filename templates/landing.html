Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about FamPilot

Do I need to download an app?

No. FamPilot works in your phone's browser. You can add it to your home screen for an app-like experience — no app store needed.

How does the family sharing work?

When you create a family, you get an invite code and QR code. Share it with your family members — they scan or enter the code and instantly see the same lists, chores, and events. No emails or accounts required.

How does the photo-to-calendar feature work?

Take a photo of any event flyer, school notice, or schedule. FamPilot's AI reads the image and extracts the event title, date, time, and location. You review it and add it to Google Calendar with one tap.

What can the voice commands do?

The AI voice agent can add items to shopping lists, mark chores as done, create calendar events, assign chores to family members, and generate meal plans — all from a single voice command.

How does AI meal planning work?

Tell FamPilot your family size and any dietary preferences. The AI generates a full week of breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks with prep times. It also creates a grocery list you can send straight to your shared shopping list.

Is FamPilot free?

Yes. The free plan includes 5 AI scans per month, unlimited shared lists, chore tracking, and voice commands. The Pro plan ($5/month per family) adds unlimited AI scans and priority processing.
